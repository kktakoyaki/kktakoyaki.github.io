Commission Info

General Rules

  • Will Do: Will draw almost anything. Fanarts, OCs, OCs with characters from another series, furries (as for scalies, I admit I’ve never drawn one, but I’m super excited to try ᕕ( ᐛ )ᕗ let’s gooo), NSFW, violence, gore, certain specific fetishes.
  • Will Do, with extra charge: Multiple characters / group picture, detailed weapons and armors, complex machinery, details to the composition such as complex foreground and background.
  • Won’t Do: Ideas I’m not comfortable with, such as some certain specific fetishes or personal views on certain subject matters. Please do send me the details to your commission request, and I will let you know if I am willing to work on your commission order.
  • Payment via Paypal / Jenius only. I will send an invoice to you and will start working on the commission once I receive the payment. You may discuss payment plans with me.
  • Updates: You may contact me about the progress of your commission at any time, and I'll get back to you as soon as possible within 24 hours.
  • Privacy: I often stream my drawing process on Twitch. If you would rather keep your commission a secret, please let me know in advance so I won’t stream while working on your commission.
  • Turnaround time: Please give me two to five working days to complete your commission. If I need more time for your commission, I will let you know and give you an estimation of the time needed. Please also let me know in advance regarding deadlines.
  • Revisions: Before line work, I offer major revisions up to three times. Changing the pose and altering the layout are considered major revisions. After line work is done, I cannot offer major revisions. If you insist, extra charges will be incurred accordingly. After coloring is done, the only minor changes I can offer you are color corrections and minor decorative additions to the picture. I may charge you for more revisions depending on how major the changes are / how many changes are made.

Terms and Conditions

  1. As the commissioner, feel free to post / share the commissioned art anywhere. It is yours.
    However, please credit CAKWE as the artist. Please do not claim that you drew it yourself.

  2. As the commissioner, you may use and edit the commissioned art as icons/banners.
    Cropping, resizing, and color correcting the art by using additional filters is allowed.

  3. If you plan to use the art for commercial purposes, please let me know in advance.
    The prices I’ve stated in this website are only for personal-use artworks.

Contact Information

When contacting me about your commission, please include at least 2 of the following:

  • References of the character(s)
  • Character’s facial expression
  • References of additional objects
  • Sample poses and other object placement (even a stickman drawing really helps me)
  • Image description (what are they doing? overall mood of the picture? other composition details)
  • Anything else I need to pay extra attention to? Example: additional markings, scar, tattoo, birthmark, jewelries, clothing detail, license plate on a car, etc
The details may be in writing or image, or a both. Please feel free to ask me if you have any questions, or need me to clarify more!
